Our Associations with Industry Leaders

Grains Global’s collaborations with key organizations like SGCCI, NACOF, and ICAR are shaping a fair, transparent agricultural marketplace. By supporting buyers, partners and AgroPreneurs and bridging gaps in the supply chain, we foster innovation, growth, and global prosperity in agriculture.

The Southern Gujarat Chamber of Commerce and Industry has been serving the industry of South Gujarat since 1940 with focus on promoting trade, commerce, and industry. With various initiatives such as trade fairs, business networking events, training programs, and policy advocacy on business-related issues, SGCCI is fostering a behest for growth and excellence in business community. Surat’s rise to prominence as the world’s diamond-cutting centre and India’s most prominent man-made textile industry, has put the city on the global map, empowering and facilitating business expansion across the world.

ICAR-Agricultural Technology Application Research Institute, Kolkata (Zone-V) The network of 731 Krishi Vigyan Kendras spread across the country is the part of Division of agricultural Extension of ICAR. Deputy Director General (AE) who looks after the administrative, financial and overall functioning of KVK. Agricultural Technology Application Research Institutes (ATARIs) are looking after monitoring the KVK system in the state and district level. The Division of Agricultural Extension of ICAR is supported by eight erstwhile Zonal Project Directorates (now Agricultural Technology Application Research Institutes (ATARIs). The objective of the Institute is to plan, monitor, evaluate and guide and monitor the programmes of the KVK and judge the performance of KVKs time to time.

National Federation of Farmers’ Procurement, Processing and Retailing Cooperatives of India Ltd, (NACOF) has been established under the Multi-State Cooperative Societies Act 2002 and registered on 4th November 2009 by the Joint Secretary & Central Registrar, Cooperative Societies in the Ministry of Agriculture, Govt. of India at Registration No. MSCS/CR/320/2009. The membership of NACOF is 74 comprising of the State Level Federations, Distt Level Cooperative Societies, and large size/primary level societies from 25 States and UTs of India i.e. the States of Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Bihar, Maharashtra, West Bengal, Chhattisgarh, Uttarakhand, Odisha, Rajasthan, Jharkhand, Himachal Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Karnataka, Kerala, Punjab, Arunachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Assam, Manipur, Chandigarh, Tamil Nadu, and Haryana. Copies of the registration certificates are appended.
